To ensure a smooth application process, please follow these guidelines:

  1. Fill in all required fields accurately. Incorrect information can delay processing.
  2. Use your full name as it appears on official documents.
  3. Provide your current residential address and ensure it's up to date.
  4. Input a valid contact number and email address for communication purposes.
  5. Select your gender from the dropdown menu accurately.
  6. Enter your Learner's Reference Number (LRN) if applicable.
  7. Indicate your current grade level accurately.
  8. Provide your parent or guardian's name and contact number for verification.
  9. Review the information you've provided before submitting the form.
  10. Check the consent box to agree to the Terms and Privacy Policy.
  11. Keep your information confidential; do not share your details with others.

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to contact our support team.
